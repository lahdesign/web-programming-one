Google Fonts

To use Google Fonts:

  1. Browse the font collection at www.google.com/fonts
  2. Once you’ve selected a font, click the blue “Add to Collection” button
  3. In the pane at the bottom of the screen, click “Use.”
  4. Follow the instructions on the page.
